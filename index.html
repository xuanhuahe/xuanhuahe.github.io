About Me

I am current a Master's student at the University of Science and Technology of China, advised by Professors Jie Zhang and Chengjun Xie. I earned my Bachelor's in Software Engineering in 2022 from Xiamen University, under the guidance of Professor Yongxuan Lai. During my undergraduate studies, I also engaged in research at the MOCOM lab.

My research interests are centered around computer vision, specifically in image super-resolution, enhancement, and video generation. Previously, I have explored remote sensing image processing and federated learning. My goal is to make the world looks beautiful and clear.
