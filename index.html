About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Prof. Qifeng Chen. I received my M.S. from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), advised by Professors Jie Zhang and Chengjun Xie. I earned my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering in 2022 from Xiamen University, under the guidance of Prof. Yongxuan Lai, and conducted research at the MOCOM Lab.

My current research focuses on video generation and world models—including long-horizon, controllable, and identity-consistent video synthesis. Previously, I worked on low-level vision problems such as image restoration.
